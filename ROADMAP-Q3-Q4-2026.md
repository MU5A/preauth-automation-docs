# SaasPro Labs Roadmap: Q3-Q4 2026

## Overview

This document outlines SaasPro Labs' strategic priorities for the next 2-3 quarters following our seed investment. The focus is on infrastructure stability, regulatory compliance, market expansion, and global presence.

---

## Phase 1: Infrastructure & Operations (Month 1-2)

| Service | Purpose | Priority |
|---------|---------|----------|

---

## Phase 2: Regulatory Compliance (Month 2-3)

### NDPR Compliance

1. **Engage Nigerian Auditing Firm**
   - Data protection impact assessment
   - Privacy policy review
   - Consent mechanism audit
   - Staff training requirements

2. **NDPR Registration**
   - File with NITDA (National Information Technology Development Agency)
   - Obtain compliance certificate

3. **Documentation**
   - Data processing agreements with HMO partners
   - Patient data handling procedures
   - Breach notification protocols

**Why this matters:** Healthcare data is sensitive. NDPR compliance is non-negotiable for operating in Nigeria and builds trust with enterprise HMO clients.

**Objectives:**

- [ ] Explore strategic partnerships

**Northstar Startup Program:**
- Apply for exhibition space
- Pitch competition entry
- Media and PR opportunities

### UAE Company Registration

**Structure:** Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC) or ADGM Free Zone

**Benefits:**
- 0% corporate tax
- 100% foreign ownership
- Gateway to GCC healthcare markets
- Credibility for international investors

**Requirements:**
- Local registered agent
- Virtual/physical office
- Initial capital deposit
